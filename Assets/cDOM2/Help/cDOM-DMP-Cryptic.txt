Changes Made from UT99 to UT2004
--------------------------------
Enhanced particle Fx, new sounds, no UT99 textures reused, new textures, 'New lava'a la UT2004, new outdoor
environment, adrenaline added, health vials added, bot paths & weapon ammo tweaked w/new jumpspots, use of 
standard weapon bases (some with minor texture changes), and a few new static meshes.

Level Comments
--------------
My aim was to replicate as much as possible of the original Cryptic.  Since UT99 textures were 8-bit and
quite small, there reuse was not practical, so new textures were either made from scratch or replaced
with UT2004 "stock" textures that were similar.  Since brush porting of UT99 to UT2004 isn't the cleanest
in the world, meshes had to be used in specific areas as needed to elimnate HOMs. The lower corridor was
embelished a bit on my part and left the orignal just a bit as this section became my creative output.
